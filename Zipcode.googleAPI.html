Communities thrive when residents are healthy. Part of public health is making wellness services easily accessible to residents. This app will provide a central location for residents to find pharmacies where they can receive vaccinations, fitness classes, and public outdoor spaces to engage in physical activity. These resources can be browsed or filtered by location or type.
